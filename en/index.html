Frequently Asked Questions

What Everyone Asks

Do I have to pay anything to talk with Jennifer?+
No. The consultation is 100% FREE. And if we connect you with an attorney, you don't pay anything upfront — only if you win.
What if I don't have papers or immigration status?+
In New York, accident victims have legal rights REGARDLESS of immigration status. Your information is 100% private.
How long do I have to file my case?+
Each type of case has a specific time limit to begin. Call us immediately and we'll advise you on your particular situation. Every day that passes is less evidence available.
What if I was partly at fault?+
You can still win your case in NY. The law allows claims even if you were partially responsible.
Are you attorneys?+
No. GANASTE connects injured people with certified NY attorneys. Jennifer is Case Coordinator and Legal Advisor.
What areas of NY do you cover?+
We serve all 5 NYC boroughs (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, Staten Island), Long Island, and Westchester.
